    Welcome to the Todo Tab App! Effortlessly manage tasks with this 'New Tab' page. Keep track of your to-do list, cultivate daily habits, and maintain organized thoughts in the notes section.

    Key features include:

    • An editable name, allowing you to personalize your experience.
    • Local storage for persistent data, ensuring your settings and tasks are saved between sessions.
    • A daily quote to inspire and motivate you.
    • Automatic refresh of a new image from Unsplash, providing visual inspiration.

    Stay productive, stay organized, and make the most of every day with the Todo Tab App!

    This app is responsive, but optimal desktop screen width is 1380px and higher. In the Todo list: left click (or press on mobile devices) to toggle completed, and right click (or long press on mobile devices) to delete todo. Please note that your data is stored locally in the browser of the device you are currently using. It won't be accessible on another device.
